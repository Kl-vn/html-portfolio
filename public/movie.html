The Best Movies According to Kelvin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

All About Who You Know

I specifically enjoyed this movie because of the unconventional plot.
It was pretty low budget but the film has an ebb and flow to it, I must've seen it countless times.

Past Lives

I saw this movie and I thought it was really great, great love story about how we may not end up with a person who we're really connected to, someone seemingly a soulmate.
However, we may still end up with someone we love. Very well written movie.

Entergalactic

This, is just a feel good animation, with a cliche but very acceptable storyline and modern/hip cultural references. It also has a really cool soundtrack, which I listen to occasionally on Spotify.
